The restaurant is located at the Gräsöbaden campsite with a magnificent view of the sea.
Simple but well-prepared food is served here.
Choose between a fantastic a la carte, pizza or a good drink at the outdoor terrace directly by the sea.
With us, everyone is welcome, as you wish, vacationers in flip flops, families with children who want to get their fill after a day at the beach, or celebrants who want something special.
A large guest jetty is available for those of you who arrive by boat.
